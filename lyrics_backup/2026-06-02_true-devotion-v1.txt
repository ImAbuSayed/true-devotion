[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Caught in the pull of a quiet gaze
A midnight fire through a smoky haze
Pulling you close in the fading light
Lost in the rhythm of a restless night

[Verse 1]
Two shadows dancing on a wooden floor
I never knew I could want you more
Your breath is warm on my weary skin
This is the moment where we begin

[Pre-Chorus]
Every whisper is a spark in the dark
Leaving a beautiful, permanent mark
Slowly falling into deep surrender
Nothing has ever felt quite this tender

[Chorus]
I am giving you my true devotion
Drowning deep in a heavy emotion
Hold on tight as the world slows down
We are the only ones left in this town

[Post-Chorus]
(Only ones left, yeah)
We are the only ones left in this town

[Verse 2]
Tracing the lines on your open palm
Finding a storm right inside the calm
Every secret that you softly share
Tangles me up in your midnight hair

[Chorus]

[Post-Chorus 2]

[Bridge]
Shadows fade as the morning nears
Washing away all our hidden fears
Every heartbeat is a ringing bell
You have Saeed under a perfect spell
Pouring my soul in the midnight air
A true devotion beyond compare

[Spoken]
Look into my eyes and stay a while
Let me get lost in your beautiful smile

[Final Chorus]
